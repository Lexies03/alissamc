ABOUT ME

ALISSA MAE CELESTE

          My name is Alissa Mae Celeste, 23 years old, a computer engineering graduate from Surigao del Norte State University (SNSU). I was born and raised in Surigao City. I was also one of the first batches of the new curriculum K-12 program where I took the course IT in Mobile App and Web Development. Since I was a child, I really love being with computers. I was just amazed at how the computer worked and how it was built. I love everything about it. So I dreamed to have a job related to computers.
     When I entered senior high school, my love of computers grew. During those years, programming was introduced to me. I really don't have an idea what programming is. However, when our teacher discussed and show the learning outcome, I was amazed and at the same time got curious about it. While starting programming, I find myself enjoying it. And when I finish something, like when I finished a user interface, It amazes me and I feel fulfilled whenever I finished one. It feels like magic to me. From there, my dream got bigger and now I am currently attending a FullStack Web Development Bootcamp at Uplift Code Camp training school based in Quezon City, Philippines.
